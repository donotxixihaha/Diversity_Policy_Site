What is Diversity Policy?

Diversity Policy is a database of policy documents and information from colleges and universities in the United States. The service is continually expanding with new policy entries every day.

Contribute to Our Database

Diversity Policy strives to foster a community of policy researchers and human resource professionals, working in tandem to add to a database of policy information with high utility-value. For more information on how to get involved, please contact us at: info@diversitypolicy.com.
